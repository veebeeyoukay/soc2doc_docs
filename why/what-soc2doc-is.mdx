You have the headache, or you don't. If you do, hand it over.

soc2doc is a problem-transfer service for SOC 2. You hand off the whole thing —
the complexity, the parts you do not know how to do, the auditor relationship you
have not built — and a 27-year practitioner takes it. You go back to shipping the
product only your team can ship.

## What it is

- **A painkiller, not a vitamin.** You are already in pain — the audit is looming,
  or the report is issued and now something has to be true about it every quarter.
  soc2doc kills the pain you have now. It is not a "get a little better at
  compliance over time" product.
- **Transfer, not coaching.** We take it off your plate. We do not hand you a
  course, a checklist, and a wish of good luck. The knowledge, the sequence, and the
  auditor calls are ours to carry.
- **Backed by a practitioner.** 27 years, 40+ SOC 2 certifications led, 150+
  organizations served. A person, named, who has sat on the auditor's side of the
  table.

## What it is not

- **Not the auditor.** soc2doc never performs the audit. SOC 2 audits are performed
  by an independent CPA firm. We prepare you, help you pick the right auditor, and
  sit on the calls — the audit report comes from your auditor, and independence
  stays intact.
- **Not a gap analysis in the free review.** The free [Commitment Review](/product/commitment-review)
  mirrors your report; it does **not** grade your organization. Judging whether you
  meet your commitments is a paid engagement.
- **Not a dashboard you operate.** There is no console for you to log into and
  babysit. The point of handing it over is that you stop operating it.

## Who it is for

The dependable person other people are counting on — the founder, CTO, or
compliance lead who has made commitments and keeps them, and who is stuck this
week between "who I am" and "what I can actually get to." If that is not you, none
of this lands, and that is fine. We do not spend a word convincing anyone they are
in pain.
